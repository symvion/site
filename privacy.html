Privacy Policy

📅 Last Updated: May 2026 | 🇬🇷 SYMVION (Concept / Idea Phase)

This Privacy Policy describes how SYMVION ("we," "our," "the Company," or "the Concept") handles information during our pre-launch, idea-validation, market research, and investor pitching phase. We are committed to transparency, data minimalism, and responsible innovation.

🔒 At a glance:
• No user accounts · No database · No tracking cookies
• All assessments run locally in your browser
• We only receive what you voluntarily email us
• Your information is never sold, rented, or shared

1. Information We Collect

Since SYMVION is currently in idea/pitch development stage (no live software platform deployed), we collect only what you voluntarily provide:

  • Email communications — name, email address, company name, and message content when you contact us at symvion.gr@gmail.com
  • Business Readiness Scorecard answers — processed anonymously and locally in your web browser; no data is transmitted to any server
  • Basic analytics — standard page visit counts (no cookies, no personal identifiers, no third-party tracking scripts)

2. How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries about our business concept, partnership opportunities, or investor relations
  • To validate market interest and refine our service design (aggregated, anonymized insights only)
  • To improve our pitch deck, website demo, and presentation materials
  • To communicate with potential co-founders, advisors, or early-stage beta testers

3. Data Storage & Security

We do not operate a production database or backend infrastructure. Any information submitted via email resides solely in our email inbox (Google Workspace / Gmail, protected by industry-standard security). The Business Readiness Scorecard runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript — no data leaves your device. We implement reasonable safeguards for email communications, but please understand this is a demonstration environment as we prepare for formal launch.

4. Third-Party Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any personal information with third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated patterns (e.g., "37% of demo users reported unclear requirements") with potential investors or advisors under non-disclosure agreements, but never any personal identifiers such as names or email addresses.

5. Your Rights & Control

Because no permanent user accounts or databases exist, there is no personal data to delete or correct. If you have emailed us and wish to have your message permanently removed, simply reply to any thread or send a request to symvion.gr@gmail.com, and we will delete the entire conversation within 5 business days.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies, localStorage tracking, fingerprinting, or any persistent identifiers. The only browser storage used is temporary JavaScript variables for the scorecard assessment, which disappear when you close the page.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services, concept, and demonstration website are directed exclusively toward business professionals, entrepreneurs, investors, and enterprise decision-makers aged 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect any information from minors.

8. International Visitors

Our email communications are processed in Greece and the European Union. By contacting us, you acknowledge that your information will be transmitted to and processed in Greece, which may have different data protection laws than your country of residence. We comply with Greek and EU privacy standards as applicable to pre-launch entities.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

As SYMVION evolves from an idea/concept into a live service or funded venture, this Privacy Policy will be materially updated. You will be notified of any changes via a prominent notice on our website. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy will always reflect the most recent revision.
